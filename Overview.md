# OGV / NLLP — Digital Presence

## Overview

This project is the official digital presence for Ocean Green Ventures (OGV) and NLLP — a healthcare consultancy focused on strategic infrastructure development, operational advisory, and long-term healthcare ecosystem planning.

The website is designed to communicate:

- Strategic clarity
- Architectural thinking
- Operational precision
- Modern healthcare leadership

This is not a typical corporate website.
It is a motion-driven, editorial, and immersive brand experience.

---

## Core Philosophy

The site reflects the way OGV / NLLP operates:

- Strategy first
- Execution grounded in structure
- Calm authority over loud marketing
- Precision over decoration

The digital experience emphasizes depth, space, and controlled motion to mirror the consultancy’s methodical approach to healthcare development.

---

## Technical Stack

- Next.js (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- GSAP + ScrollTrigger
- Lenis (smooth scroll)
- Structured component architecture

The stack is chosen to allow cinematic motion while maintaining SEO, scalability, and long-term maintainability.

---

## Experience Design Principles

1. Motion with Purpose  
   Every animation must reinforce hierarchy or narrative.

2. Space as Authority  
   Generous whitespace communicates confidence.

3. Depth as Metaphor  
   Foreground vs background motion represents strategy vs execution.

4. Restraint Over Trend  
   No excessive gradients, no neon aesthetics, no artificial effects.

5. Performance First  
   Smooth, stable, and production-ready.
