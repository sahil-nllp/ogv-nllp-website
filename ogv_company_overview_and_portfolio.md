# Ocean Green Ventures (OGV)

## About OGV

Ocean Green Ventures (OGV) is a private, independent boutique venture studio and product development partner. OGV specialises in building, scaling, and commercialising ventures through structured execution models.

OGV applies a methodical, sprint-based approach to venture development, enabling predictable costs, transparent processes, and measurable outcomes. The organisation supports startups, scaleups, research institutions, and government bodies across Australia, New Zealand, and international markets.

---

## Core Philosophy

OGV standardises and itemises the working components of a venture. Every engagement is broken into defined tools, structured sprints, and clear deliverables. This allows clients to:

- Choose only the tools they need
- Select preferred teams
- Maintain transparency in delivery
- Cancel or renew sprint cycles flexibly
- Receive all developed assets upon completion

Each sprint represents 30 days of focused execution.

## Service Offerings

### 1. Development as a Service (DAAS)

### 2. Teams as a Service (TAAS)
Dedicated, scalable technical and product teams deployed to ventures.

Specialist talent acquisition and research collaboration services, particularly for deep-tech and research initiatives.
